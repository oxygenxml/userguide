Space Ships

Specifications and details about the X22000 and Z11000 space crafts

A space ship is a vehicle or machine designed to fly in outer space. Space ships are used for a variety of purposes, including communications, Earth observation, meteorology, navigation, space colonization, planetary exploration, and transportation of humans and cargo. All space ships, except single-stage-to-orbit vehicles, cannot get into space on their own, and require a launch vehicle (carrier rocket).
